Join the Crypto Gaming Revolution

In the Age of Crypto, the world economy runs on a universal blockchain. Competing factions vie for network superiority, employing sophisticated cyber attacks. In this hyper capitalist society, there are no governments to maintain order, and the only law is survival of the fittest.

Be a Part of History

The future of online gaming is here, and it is decentralized. Player-owned games are the next step in the evolution of massive multiplayer gaming, made possible through blockchain technology.

Worldwide Competition and Collaboration

In Age of Crypto, players from New York to Hong Kong compete on a single, worldwide map. To survive against myriad opponents you must forge alliances and trading partnerships. A winning strategist is equal parts politician and military tactician.

Upgrade Your Nodes

As you mine tokens, use them to upgrade your offensive abilities with viruses. Bolster your defenses with firewalls. Add GPUs to increase mining capacity and research new technologies to stay ahead of the curve.

Powered By Ethereum

Each asset in Age of Crypto is a unique, non-fungible crypto asset with real economic value. The rules of the game are enshrined in smart contracts, so you can be sure that gameplay is fair and equitable.
